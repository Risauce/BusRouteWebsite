FAQ

Got Questions? We have answers!

  • WHAT IS THE COST? It's free! There is absoulutely no cost to riding the bus.

  • HOW DO I KNOW WHERE MY NEARBY PICK UP LOCATIONS ARE? We hold all the route maps on our schedule page, check them out!

  • IS IT HANDICAP ACCESSABLE? Yes it is! There are lifts on the buses that work great.

  • ARE THERE SAFETY CONCERNS? The buses drive safely during the winter, there is a dedicated place for lost items, and all our drivers are professionally tested.

  • DO YOU ALLOW SERVICE ANIMALS? Yes, the buses allow service animals. We also allow small animals that can be consigned to a lap case carrier.

About Us

We created this site because we believed there was a need for a guide/tip page to explain the amazing transportation system that is the Streamline Service in Bozeman. We are just a simple group of undergraduates at Montana State University that are building this website (and probably not publishing it for several reasons) to help out those who may ever get confused about the streamline bus service. This is only because there are several reasons that one could get confused about it, as we touch on throughout our site. Thanks for reading!

Why Take the Bus?

Your alternatives are almot nonexistant when compared to the Streamline Bus System. It allows college students, commuters, and all other citizens of the Bozeman/Livingston area to enjoy free transportation anywhere they may need.

Pros

  • Cost Effective (FREE!)

  • Efficient Routes, 24/7

  • Seatbelts - Safe!

  • Quick and easy Transportation

  • Sustainable.

  • Who needs a car? Now you don't!

Cons

  • Very public.

  • It's completely free, you don't even need to pay.

  • It can't take you anywhere in America.

  • Paid with Tax and Tuition dollars.

  • Don't allow all animals, transport your python another way.

  • Sometimes the scheduling is ruined and your bus doesn not arrive on time.
