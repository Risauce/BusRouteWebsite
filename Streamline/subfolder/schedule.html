Free Travel

The bus is free to ride on for all people of Bozeman and Livingston. This service is a free and cheap alternative to driving, and can also relieve the cost of a parking pass if you take it to school.

Streamline Bus

Streamline offers fare-free service throughout the Bozeman area to fulfill everyone’s transportation needs. Our routes provide service to and from Bozeman, Belgrade, Four Corners, Livingston and seasonal routes to Bridger Bowl. Streamline also connects with the the Skyline Bus System for travel to and from Big Sky.

How to Ride the Bus

Stop at the bus stop next to the Strand Student Union Building. The bus will pick up and drop off from here. You can step on and follow any of the routes detailed below. The operating hours are also given below.
